# LIBRARY POLICIES

## POLICY: Borrowing Rules

Policy Name: Borrowing Rules

Details:

* Members may borrow up to 5 books simultaneously.
* Loan period is 14 days.
* Reference materials cannot be borrowed.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

## POLICY: Renewal Rules

Policy Name: Renewal Rules

Details:

* Books may be renewed twice.
* Renewals are not allowed if another member has reserved the book.
* Renewal requests must be made before the due date.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

## POLICY: Fine Rules

Policy Name: Fine Rules

Details:

* Overdue books incur a fine of $0.50 per day.
* Lost books must be replaced or paid for at current market value.
* Fines exceeding $20 may result in account suspension.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

## POLICY: Reservation Rules

Policy Name: Reservation Rules

Details:

* Members may reserve unavailable books.
* Reserved books are held for 3 days after notification.
* Maximum of 3 active reservations per member.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

## POLICY: Membership Rules

Policy Name: Membership Rules

Details:

* Membership requires valid identification.
* Membership is valid for one year.
* Members must keep contact information up to date.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY
