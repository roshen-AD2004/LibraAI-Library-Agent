## LIBRARY POLICIES

### POLICY: Borrowing Rules

Policy Name: Borrowing Rules

Details:

* Members may borrow up to 5 books simultaneously.
* Standard loan period is 14 days.
* Some books may allow a 21-day loan period.
* Reference materials cannot be borrowed.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

### POLICY: Renewal Rules

Policy Name: Renewal Rules

Details:

* Books may be renewed twice.
* Renewals are not allowed if another member has reserved the book.
* Renewal requests must be made before the due date.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

### POLICY: Fine Rules

Policy Name: Fine Rules

Details:

* Regular Books: $0.50 per overdue day.
* New Releases: $2.00 per overdue day.
* Premium Members receive a 50% fine discount.
* Lost books must be replaced or paid for at market value.
* Fines above $20 may result in account suspension.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

### POLICY: Reservation Rules

Policy Name: Reservation Rules

Details:

* Members may reserve unavailable books.
* Reserved books are held for 3 days after notification.
* Maximum 3 active reservations per member.
* Reservations are processed in queue order.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

### POLICY: Membership Rules

Policy Name: Membership Rules

Details:

* Membership requires valid identification.
* Membership is valid for one year.
* Members must keep contact information updated.
* Membership tiers include Standard and Premium.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

---

### TIER: Standard Membership

Tier Name: Standard Membership

Details:

* Borrow up to 5 books
* 14-day loan period (21 days for selected titles)
* 2 active reservations allowed
* Standard fine rates apply
* Email notifications for due dates

Source Section: MEMBERSHIP_TIER

---

### TIER: Premium Membership

Tier Name: Premium Membership

Details:

* Borrow up to 10 books
* Extended loan periods (21 days standard)
* 5 active reservations allowed
* 50% discount on all fines
* Priority access to new releases
* Unlimited renewals
* Priority customer support
* Exclusive member events

Source Section: MEMBERSHIP_TIER

---

### POLICY: Account Suspension

Policy Name: Account Suspension

Details:

* Accounts are suspended if fines exceed $20.
* Accounts may be suspended for non-return of materials.
* Suspension prevents all borrowing privileges.
* Contact library staff to reinstate account.

Source Section: LIBRARY_POLICY

## OPERATIONAL SCENARIOS

These scenarios are intentionally included in the knowledge base to provide the agent with realistic examples of how the library operates and how staff should respond. Use them for context when answering operational questions.

Scenario: Mixed Availability
- Dune: Some copies are available while others are checked out; reservation queue exists.
- Atomic Habits: All copies currently checked out; reservation queue exists; users may request notification when available.

Scenario: Overdue & Fines
- A member who returns a book late will receive an overdue notice and a fine according to the Fine Rules. Premium members receive a 50% discount.

Scenario: Disputes & Escalation
- If a member disputes a fine or reports returning a book, the agent should record the dispute in the transaction log and advise the member to contact library staff for escalation.

Scenario: Renewals & Restrictions
- Renewals are allowed unless another member has reserved the book. Renewals may be limited by membership tier.

Scenario: Reservation Notifications
- When a reserved book becomes available, the system notifies the next member in the queue and holds the book for 3 days.

Source Section: OPERATIONAL_SCENARIOS
